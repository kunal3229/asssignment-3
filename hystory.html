History of the game


The game of basketball was first played on the lands of New England, when a young physical education professor, Canadian James Naismith was thinking of ways to keep his class active and fit during the winter. He simply nailed a peach basket to a 3m long track and set a few ground rules. Who knew that this little creation would have millions of fans some day?!
Frank Mahan, who also happens to be one of the original players of the game, spoke to Naismith back in 1892 to understand his plans for the game in the future. “Call it Naismith ball”, he suggested. James laughed and said, “Why not basketball? We have a ball and a basket!”
The first men’s National championship tournament was organized in 1937 and the gave has been a hit since then!
